## GoHighLevel

App name: Boltcall

Short description: AI speed-to-lead calling and SMS follow-up for HighLevel agencies.

Tagline: AI speed-to-lead calling and SMS follow-up for local service businesses.

Description: Boltcall is an AI speed-to-lead system for local service businesses and the agencies that serve them. When a new lead comes from a workflow, form, ad, contact event, or CRM automation, Boltcall starts immediate phone and SMS follow-up so the business can respond before competitors do.

Use Boltcall to trigger first-touch outreach, qualify intent, capture source and status, and keep the customer record aligned inside the operating CRM. It is built for teams where response speed directly affects booked jobs: dental, construction, home services, med spas, legal, and other local-service verticals.

Core workflows include sending new contacts to Boltcall, creating or updating CRM contacts from Boltcall leads, tagging contacts with Boltcall source data, and routing urgent leads into instant AI calling or texting. The CRM stays the system of record, while Boltcall becomes the instant-response layer.

## App Concept

Boltcall for Zoho CRM sends new leads and contacts into Boltcall for instant AI phone and SMS follow-up, then writes outcomes back to Zoho CRM.

## Recommended OAuth Scopes

- Read Zoho CRM leads and contacts.
- Create/update notes or tasks for Boltcall outcomes.
- Optional: update custom fields for first-touch status, call started, booked status, and source.

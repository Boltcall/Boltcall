## App Concept

Boltcall for Pipedrive turns new leads, people, and deals into instant speed-to-lead calls and SMS follow-up.

## Recommended OAuth Scopes

- Read leads, people, organizations, deals, activities, and users.
- Write activities or notes for Boltcall outcomes.
- Optional: write lead/deal custom fields for first-touch status and source.

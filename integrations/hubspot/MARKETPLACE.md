# HubSpot Marketplace Submission Notes

## Listing

**App name:** Boltcall

**Short description:** Call, text, and book new HubSpot leads instantly with AI speed-to-lead automation.

**Description:** Boltcall helps local service businesses respond to every new lead immediately. Connect HubSpot to sync contacts into Boltcall, trigger instant phone/SMS follow-up, and write outcomes back into the CRM so teams know which leads were contacted, qualified, booked, or need follow-up.

## Required later work

- OAuth app configuration.
- HubSpot workflow action: `Trigger Boltcall speed-to-lead`.
- Optional CRM card/app event surface for call outcomes.
- Marketplace screenshots and demo video.

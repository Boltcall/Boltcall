# Make Marketplace Submission Notes

## Listing

**App name:** Boltcall

**Short description:** Instantly respond to local-service leads from Make scenarios.

**Description:** Boltcall is an AI speed-to-lead system for local service businesses. The Make app lets teams send leads from forms, ad platforms, CRMs, sheets, and webhook flows into Boltcall, then use Boltcall lead events to update the rest of their stack.

## Review checklist

- Working API-key connection.
- `Watch Leads` returns stable `id` values for deduplication.
- `Create Lead` accepts email or phone and returns the created lead.
- `Find Lead` scopes results to the authenticated workspace.
- Screenshots for connection, Create Lead mapping, and Watch Leads output.
